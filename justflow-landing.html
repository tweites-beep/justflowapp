The Framework

Four steps to unclockable.

S
Set Context
Tell the AI who it is and exactly what situation it's working in. Context is the foundation of everything.
N
Name the Ask
Be specific about exactly what you want produced. Precision at this stage changes the entire output.
A
Aim the Goal
Define the purpose and result you want to achieve. The AI needs to know exactly where it's going.
